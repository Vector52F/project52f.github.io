Buying the Floor

Unlike protocols that inject native tokens (increasing supply), the Dampener injects QF—the paired asset. This is mathematically equivalent to buying 52F from the market and burning it, except the tokens remain in the pool, deepening liquidity without diluting holders.

The 10,400 QF vault reserve (20% of seed) provides immediate firepower during the critical Yellow Zone, while accumulated taxes from the Engine provide sustained defense in the Green Zone.
