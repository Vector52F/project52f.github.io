The Collision Engine

Probability.
Not Luck.

Qualifying To Enter

Not every buy earns a collision slot. Two gates must pass on the same transaction: you must hold ≥52,000,000 tokens after your buy is credited, and your buy must be ≥52,000,000 tokens in a single transaction.

The Epoch (52 Participants)

An epoch fills when 52 qualifying buyers register. Each wallet is mapped to a bucket number (0–999) by hashing the first 8 bytes of the address and taking modulo 1000. The first bucket where two or more addresses collide is the winner.

The Must-Still-Hold Gate

Before prizes transfer, each winner is checked again. If a winner sold tokens between entering and the flush, they fail. Their share rolls into the rollover pot, rewarding those who held. Selling before payout actively rewards everyone who didn't.

Collision Probability
52 participants across 1,000 buckets = ~73% chance of collision per epoch.
