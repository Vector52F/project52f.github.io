How It Works

QF In. 52F Out.
Mathematical Recycling.

Every transaction generates QF tax. This QF is used to buy 52F from the market for prize refills and buybacks, creating persistent upward pressure on 52F price. The system is self-sustaining: volume generates QF, QF buys 52F, 52F prizes drive more volume.
